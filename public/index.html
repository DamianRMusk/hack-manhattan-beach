AngelHacks




The first high school Hack Club in Manhattan Beach, Los Angeles.




Why do we exist?




We believe that programming is the closest skill that one can have to a superpower:
the ability to fully utilize devices that are ancestors would view as magical is easily
among the best skillsets that anyone in our rapidly-moving world could have.

Therefore, our goal is to take those who are even slightly curious or interested in the field
and nurture their interest to its full potential through thorough and interesting curricula
and software projects built from scratch.
